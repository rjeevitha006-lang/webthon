    • About Rajarajeshwari Dental College & Hospital

    Vision

    To deliver high-quality dental education and promote excellence in clinical practice, research, and community service.

    Mission

    To create skilled and ethical dental professionals through modern teaching and advanced clinical training.

    Goal

    To achieve global standards in dental education by integrating academics with practical exposure.

    Our Objective

    To develop innovative, responsible, and competent professionals in dental sciences.

    About College

    Established in 1992 in Bangalore, Rajarajeshwari Dental College is recognized by DCI and affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences.

    The college offers BDS and MDS programs with modern infrastructure, advanced labs, and experienced faculty.
