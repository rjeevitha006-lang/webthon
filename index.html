    About College & Hospital

    Rajarajeswari Dental College & Hospital, established in 1992, is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in dental education and healthcare. It is recognized by DCI and affiliated with the Government of Karnataka and Government of India, providing quality training with advanced facilities.

    Transforming Smiles, Empowering Futures

    Built on a strong foundation of excellence in dental education and healthcare, Rajarajeshwari Dental College and Hospital, Bengaluru, has been a center of academic brilliance and clinical expertise since 1992. Guided by a vision to create globally competent dental professionals, the institution blends advanced learning methodologies with real-world clinical exposure.

    With affiliation to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and recognition by the Dental Council of India (DCI), the college ensures high academic standards and professional credibility. Its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and patient-centered approach create an environment where innovation meets compassion.

    From modern dental laboratories to specialized treatment units, students gain hands-on experience while serving the community. The institution emphasizes research, interdisciplinary learning, and ethical practice, preparing graduates to meet the challenges of modern dentistry with confidence.
